MULUNGUSHI MULTIDISCIPLINARY JOURNAL (MUMJ)

The Mulungushi University Multidisciplinary Journal (MUMJ) is a bi annual journal whose focus is to pursue the frontiers of knowledge. It provides a platform for sharing and communicating research and development issues from within Zambia and globally. It aspires to inform policy direction, enhance national development and transform livelihoods. The journal provides opportunities for scholars and innovators to contribute knowledge to address global challenges.

Agriculture, environment and natural resources; business law and HR; humanities and social sciences; science, engineering and ICT; technology; medicine and health sciences; education.

“To be a high quality research publications journal of integrity for sharing outstanding knowledge for Africa and globally”.

  • Provide a platform for researchers, scholars, and practitioners.
  • Promote integration of diverse perspectives to solve complex problems.

The journal welcomes interdisciplinary submissions across sciences, humanities, engineering and health sciences. Includes research articles, reviews and perspectives.

• Free access to all articles
• Creative Commons Attribution License
• Authors retain copyright
• Encourages repository sharing

Includes plagiarism, duplicate submission, citation manipulation, falsification, authorship, and conflicts of interest policies.
